What is Girls Who Code?

Girls Who Code is a national nonprofit organization dedicated towards bridging the gender gap of women in the STEM field. At girls who code, the teachers encourage us to learn code and it makes us really familiar with the basis of coding. When you go to Girls Who Code, you are a girl just like every other person there so you are in a friendly environment with people you should be comfortable with.

While coding, the teachers like to walk around to get the feeling of what you can do so they can give you the work that you should feel comfortable with so you can work calmly.

A lot of girls join, country wide, so there is a larger variety of races and grades 6th-12th. The girls at girls who code learn also how to use computers and make games. In addition, at the end of the year we make try to make an impact project to encourage other peers to join Girls Who Code.
